About "Time to Share":

What is "Time to Share"?

"Time to Share" is a "Time Banking" application. "Time Banking" is a way to invest in your loacal community. It allows one person to offer or recieve a service inexchange for "time credits". A "time credit" if a form of currency that is based on time. One time credit is worth 15 minutes.

Why is "Time to Share" important?

  • Lower residentail and economic segregation.
  • Stop the devaluation of skills and trades.
  • Prevent seclusion and loneliness.

Who should care about "Time to Share"?

Every One! A community is only as strong as its weakest link and helps everyong involved.
